Week 1 Update

The basic design of app is finalised. In basic design we have various options like 'Buy', 'Sell' and 'message box'.

Buy

Under buy section, user will see all the produts available for sale in his/her college. Every product will be categorised so that the user have ease in selecting the product of his choice. He can add the product under his wishlist if he wants to keep an eye on the product or can see the seller details and can contact him.

Once the buyer choses to buy, The seller of that product will be notified and they both can contact through phone or email and complete the exchange.

Sell

A form will be shared with the seller to fill product details like Title of product, catagory, discription and photos of the product. After submitting complete details, the product will be available to buyers in that college.

Message Box

This section will have two subsections, Inbox and sent. This will give the buyer and seller liberty to talk to each other and finalise the deal.

Setting Menu

In settings menu, user will have settings like chage contact details, change password, change email, or delete account.
